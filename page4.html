Permanent Placement Fees (once-off for an indenite period)
1 full day per week: R2,000
2 full days per week: R3,000
3 full days or more per week: 10% of the nanny’s annual salary*
*(salary x 12 ÷ 10 = placement fee)

Temporary placement fees
1 - 3 weeks (6 - 15 working days): R85 – R150 per day
3 weeks – 3 months: 25% of nanny's total earnings while with you
3 – 6 months: 15% of the nanny's total earnings while with you
6 months or more: 10% of nanny’s total annual salary.
