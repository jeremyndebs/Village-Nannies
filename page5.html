NANNY OPTIONS

DOMESTIC NANNY
Childcare and Cleaning
The nanny’s focus will be on the child(ren), but she would also need to do cleaning and laundry, depending on
how many children she is looking after and on the size of the house.
These nannies normally work 40 – 50 hours per week, depending on whether they are live-in or live-out.
R5000 – R7000 per month (R280 – R350 per day), depending on her qualications, experience, what she was
earning before and what you can afford.

CHILD CARER/MINDER
Only Childcare and some LIGHT cleaning
The child carer’s focus is mainly on the child(ren). She can also do some light domestic work , e.g. beds,
dishes, laundry, as well as cleaning and taking care of everything to do with the child(ren). Some have a
driver’s license.
These child carers normally work 40 – 50 hours per week, depending on whether they are live-in or live-out.
R6000 – R8000+ per month (R300 – R400 per day), depending on her qualications, experience, what she was
earning before and what you can afford.

TEMPORARY NANNY
We also offer temporary placements for 1 week or more.
You may need this service if:
· You are visiting Cape Town on holiday
· Your nanny is going on maternity or extended sick leave
· Your nanny is going away on her annual holiday
R280 – R450 per day, depending on her qualications, experience, what she was earning before and what you
can afford. 

HOUSEKEEPER
Cleaning and some childcare if necessary, which includes the following:
· Managing any other staff that work on the property,
· Managing repairmen, pool cleaners, etc.
· Creating shopping lists
· General domestic work and laundry
· Cooking
· Possibly looking after older children when they get home from school.
Housekeepers normally work 40 – 50 hours per week, depending on whether they are live-in or live-out.
R5000– R7000+ per month (R280 – R450 per day), depending on her qualications, experience, what she was
earning before and what you can afford.
